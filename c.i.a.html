Crafting Your Path

A One-Hour Interactive Workshop for Future Leaders

Minimum Participants: 15 students | Age Range: 14-18

Workshop Flow (60 minutes)

  • Welcome and Introduction (5 mins)
  • Your Instruments with Kerry (20 mins)
  • Your Melody with Peter (15 mins)
  • Your Orchestra with Kimber (15 mins)
  • Closing Reflections (5 mins)

1. Your Instruments with Kerry (20 mins)

Understanding Your NBI Profile

  • Interactive self-awareness exercise
  • NBI assessment introduction and importance
  • Guided interpretation of results
Impact Statistics:
  • 41% increased job satisfaction reported by students with NBI results
  • 30% increased confidence in subject selection

2. Your Melody with Peter (15 mins)

Building Meaningful Experiences

  • The value of diverse experiences
  • Embracing challenges as growth opportunities
  • Interactive discussion: "Challenge to Growth" stories

"Never underestimate the value of experience - from the extraordinary to the everyday."

3. Your Orchestra with Kimber (15 mins)

The Universe Around Us

  • Future Technology Landscape
    • Holographic technology and 3D world applications
    • AI and Quantum Photonic Technology
  • Critical Thinking and Goal Achievement
    • Strategic planning techniques
    • Time management and purposeful living
Physics in Action: Understanding projectile motion helps us grasp both space exploration and everyday movement patterns.

Closing Reflections (5 mins)

  • Key takeaways from each section
  • Personal action planning
  • Distribution of resource materials
