How It Works

Parallax Barrier Technology

V.A.R.E uses parallax barrier technology to create glasses-free 3D effects. The engine generates two slightly offset views of your video (left and right eye perspectives) and combines them using precision-controlled barrier strips that direct each view to the corresponding eye.

Technical Process

1

View Separation: Creates left and right eye perspectives by applying horizontal offset based on configurable separation distance (1-100px).

2

Barrier Generation: Renders alternating strips where odd strips show the left view and even strips show the right view, with adjustable barrier width (1-20px).

3

Depth Control: Applies depth multiplier (0.1-3.0x) to the separation offset for fine-tuning the 3D intensity.

4

Real-time Rendering: Processes video frames in real-time using elite software for immediate 3D preview and playback.
