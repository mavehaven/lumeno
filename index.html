Where it began
Origin Story

THE RAF BOT.
THE FIRST SIGNAL.

In 2022, we set out to build something that had never existed before — the RAF bot. A browser-native surveillance intelligence that could see, reason, and respond in real time. We had a vision far bigger than our resources.

No infrastructure. No team. Just a problem worth solving and the conviction that AI-powered security shouldn't require an enterprise budget. We started building anyway.

The years that followed were not quiet. Each iteration pushed the boundary of what a browser could do, what a model could see, and what a small team could deliver. The journey continues to expand.

22
RAF Bot — Genesis
First prototype of the RAF surveillance bot. Single-camera, manual zones, basic motion detection. Ran in a browser tab. Proof of concept that the idea was real.
23
Multi-Camera Expansion
Grid layout introduced. Multi-feed synchronisation, per-camera HUD overlays, and the first version of the zone editor. Scale begins to take shape.
24–25
LumenOS AI Platform
LumenOS AI Vision integration live. Real-time object recognition, confidence scoring, threat classification. The RAF bot grows into something far greater.
