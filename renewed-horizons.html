About Us

Welcome to Renewed Horizons Wellness, where we are dedicated to transforming lives through comprehensive counselling and coaching services.

Our mission is to empower individuals by providing the tools and support necessary for personal growth and well-being. At Renewed Horizons Wellness, we believe that the overarching purpose of counselling and coaching is to assist clients in devising strategies to resolve specific difficulties or current problems, such as addiction or grief.

Much like the protea flower, which symbolizes strength, courage, and resilience due to its ability to thrive in extreme conditions, our ultimate goal is to help our clients develop these same qualities. We aim to foster diversity and growth, acknowledging the unique variations and needs of each individual.

Our Services

Individual Counselling

Comprehensive assessments and individualized treatment plans to address personal challenges and mental health concerns.

Relationship Counselling

Support for couples and individuals looking to improve their relationships through open communication and conflict resolution.

Life Style Coaching

Help clients achieve personal and professional goals through personalized coaching plans and NLP techniques.

Trauma/Crisis/Violence Counselling

A safe and supportive environment for clients to share traumatic experiences with evidence-based practices.

HIV/AIDS Counselling

Specialized support for individuals living with HIV/AIDS, including coping strategies and emotional support.

Bereavement Counselling

Support for individuals coping with loss, helping them navigate the grieving process and develop coping strategies.
