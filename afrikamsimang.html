The Book

Dancing for My Father's Ashes

Navigating a blended family

"Dancing For My Father's Ashes" by Afrika Msimang is a poignant narrative that explores themes of family dynamics and betrayal. The story follows Princess, who faces a complex situation when her loving stepfather reunites with his long-lost family, leading to feelings of betrayal.

This book delves into the challenges of navigating a blended family, offering a relatable and emotional journey for readers. It offers insight into the complexities of bridging cultural divides within family dynamics, providing a thoughtful exploration of what it means to honor those who came before while forging your own path forward.

Dearest Afrika, I can finally confirm that I have now read your book. Riveting and devastating; evoking raw emotions and healing at the same time; ultimately erudite! The teaching moment for me: learn to let go of negative emotions if you don’t want to carry toxicity with you for all your life. As it sounds so personal it is easy for me to take the role of the protagonist/Princess and see life through her eyes. In this way I will take her healing as my own. I suspect that I will have many more thoughts on it as my brain works itself around how profound a writing it is. Congratulations and well done dear Afrika!
