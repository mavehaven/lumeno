Discover, Design, Develop: Your Personal Journey

A Transformative One-Hour Workshop

Facilitated by Peter, Kerry, and Tracey

For Students Ages 14-18 | Minimum Group Size: 15

Workshop Overview (60 minutes)

  • Welcome and Introduction (5 mins)
  • Self-Discovery with Tracey (20 mins)
  • Strategic Planning with Peter (20 mins)
  • Personal Branding with Kerry (10 mins)
  • Integration and Closing (5 mins)

1. Self-Discovery with Tracey (20 mins)

"Understanding Your Inner Compass"

Interactive Components:

  • Guided self-awareness exercise
  • Emotion mapping activity
  • Goal-setting framework introduction

Key Focus Areas:

  • Emotional Intelligence Development
  • Self-awareness techniques
  • Personal value identification
"Understanding yourself is the beginning of all wisdom."

2. Strategic Planning with Peter (20 mins)

"Building Your Future Blueprint"

Workshop Activities:

  • Problem-solving challenges
  • Strategic thinking exercises
  • Real-world application scenarios

Learning Outcomes:

  • Critical thinking development
  • Decision-making frameworks
  • Action planning techniques
"Experience is not what happens to you; it's what you do with what happens to you."

3. Personal Branding with Kerry (10 mins)

"Crafting Your Authentic Story"

Interactive Elements:

  • Personal brand assessment
  • Strength identification exercise
  • Communication style analysis

Key Takeaways:

  • Understanding your unique value proposition
  • Authentic self-presentation
  • Professional relationship building

Integration and Closing (5 mins)

  • Personal action plan creation
  • Resource sharing
  • Next steps and follow-up opportunities

Post-Workshop Resources

  • Digital workbook with exercises
  • Self-assessment tools
  • Recommended reading and activities
  • Contact information for mentorship opportunities
